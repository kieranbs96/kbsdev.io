Knitwell Wools

Knitwell is a well established knitting eCommerce store who have been around since 1949. The client wanted a fresh design that was usable for their audience which includes elderly customers. We decided to use Bootstrap 3 to make this possible as it's a solid framework for responsive web apps. The website was produced in the Sellerdeck Content Management system and includes a Mega Menu, Customized Checkout, Lazyload & Responsive Images. We also decided to include Feefo (Customer Review System) so that reviews could be left easily and be seen on product pages.
